System Architecture

How I Design ML Systems

I treat machine learning as a system, not a model. My designs prioritize data quality, observability, privacy, and long-term maintainability.

Typical ML System Flow

  • Ingestion: Event-driven & batch pipelines (Salesforce, APIs, streams)
  • Storage: Analytical & feature-ready layers (Snowflake, S3)
  • Modeling: Reproducible training with versioned data & models
  • Serving: Low-latency APIs or batch inference
  • Monitoring: Drift, bias, data freshness, and performance

Design Principles

  • Systems must degrade gracefully
  • Privacy is an architectural concern, not a patch
  • ML should integrate cleanly with business workflows
  • Observability beats raw accuracy in production
