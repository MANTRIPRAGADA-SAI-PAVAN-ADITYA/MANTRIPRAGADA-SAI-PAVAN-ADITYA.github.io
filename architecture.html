Design Principles

  • Data quality dominates model quality
  • Privacy is an architectural constraint
  • Monitoring is part of the product
  • Systems must degrade gracefully

How My Systems Think

  • Data is treated as a product
    Ingestion pipelines enforce contracts, validation, and freshness checks.
  • Models are replaceable components
    No system is tied to a single architecture or vendor.
  • Decisions are observable
    Every prediction is traceable to data, features, and model version.
  • Feedback is mandatory
    Drift, bias, and performance are monitored continuously.
